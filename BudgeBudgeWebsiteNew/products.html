Kanchan Oil Industries Ltd. is engaged with the production and blending of various types of edible oils and vegetable oils like Mustard Oil, Soyabean Oil, Rice Bran Oil, Til Oil and Palmolein Oil.
Our goal is to timely deliver the best quality products to our customers by assuring their performance, consistency, safety and value. We ensure that all global and local statutory and regulatory norms are met and exceeded in every phase of sourcing and procurement of best quality ingredients, blending and packaging of our products, and distribution.
