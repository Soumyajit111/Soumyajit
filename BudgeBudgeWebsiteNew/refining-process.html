Refining Processes



CPO Refining


CPO (Crude Palm Oil) refining is an essential process in the manufacturing of cooking oil. It involves the purification and enhancement of crude palm oil to produce high-quality, refined cooking oil. The refining process includes degumming, and deodorization, ensuring the removal of impurities, such as free fatty acids, color pigments, and odorous compounds. CPO refining enhances the stability, flavor, and shelf life of cooking oil, making it suitable for consumption. This refining method adheres to strict quality standards and complies with food safety regulations. By utilizing CPO refining, cooking oil manufacturing plants can consistently deliver refined cooking oil that meets consumer expectations for taste, appearance, and nutritional value.
